Sponsorship Proposal

We want to keep this event free. To make a grassroots conference like YAPC::Europe happen, we need money. If you want to give something back to the Perl community and support us, we would be happy to welcome you as a sponsor.

The website of Love of Perl 2018: http://act.yapc.eu/granada2018

Sponsorship Levels

  • Sponsorship can take many forms, such as:
  • Direct financial donations
  • Sponsorship of specific items
  • Donation of products or services for our auction
  • Help with a conference expense (we have many, so contact us for details)

We offer several sponsorship levels (details see below):

  • Gold sponsor (min. 500 €)
  • Bronze Sponsors (250€)
  • Sponsors (< 250€)

If you want to discuss some details, or propose something different, you can write us an email: jjmerelo@gmail.com

Please contact the organisers for opportunities that are not listed here. For example, we may provide a special proposal for the sponsor willing to cover one of our guest star speakers travel expenses, lunch, the attendees dinner, or the pre-conference dinner.

The following list name the items that each sponsorship level offers.

Gold Sponsors

  • One goodie distributed at the registration desk
  • Logotype on the website
  • Logotype of the company and a link to its site in printed materials the attendees receive
  • A link on the website
  • Description on the website (up to 500 characters)

Silver Sponsors

  • Logotype on the website
  • A link on the website
  • Description on the website (up to 200 characters)

Bronze Sponsors

  • Logotype on the website
  • A link on the website
  • Text on the website (up to 100 characters)

Sponsors

  • Logotype on the welcome banner
  • Logotype on the website
  • A link on the website
