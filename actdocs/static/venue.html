Conference location

Love of Perl 2018 will be held in the Carmen de la Victoria, a Carmen or Albayzin villa that belongs to the University of Granada.

The Carmen de la Victoria is located in the Albayzin quarter, right in front of the Alhambra.

Bus lines

You have to buy your ticket at the bus itself, asking for it to the driver. The common price is 1.20€.

Getting to the conference venue is quite easy using the C2 line (getting off the bus at stops labelled as "Cuesta del Chapiz"). Although it is not so far away from the city center; you can walk.

You can get more information about bus lines in the following URLs: http://www.granadadirect.com/transporte/lineas-autobuses-granada/ and http://www.transportesrober.com/ (unfortunately, both of them in Spanish).
